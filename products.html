The Luma
Rack

Enhance your space with the stylish and versatile Luma Rack. Featuring a durable gold metal frame, plush tufted seat, and built-in storage compartment, it blends elegance with practicality. Ideal for entryways, bedrooms, living rooms, or offices, it can be used as a bench, footstool, or makeup ottoman. Designed for comfort and durability, it supports up to 250 lbs.
