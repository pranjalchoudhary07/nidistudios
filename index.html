NiDi Studios is not a furniture brand. We are a design and fabrication studio — where experimental finishes meet architectural thinking, and every piece is conceived to carry an emotional weight beyond its form.

We work with luxury restaurants, boutique hotels, architects, and private collectors who understand that the objects within a space are not decorations — they are the atmosphere itself.

Material World

The textures
we love most

Brushed Stainless

Directional grain that catches light with architectural precision. Cold on touch, warm in atmosphere.

Powder Matte

Experimental color with velvet-like depth. Our most expressive finish — over 40 custom tones.

Electroplated Gold

Liquid warmth applied atom by atom. Quiet opulence without the noise of traditional gilding.

Smoked Glass

Tinted transparency that obscures and reveals simultaneously. Depth made visible through shadow.
