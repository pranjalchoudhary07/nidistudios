Material World

The metals
we love most

MS - MILD STEEL

The metal with character. Mild steel is versatile, strong, and highly workable, making it ideal for sculptural furniture and custom fabrication. It absorbs finishes beautifully and evolves with texture and depth over time. At NiDi, each piece is carefully treated and finished to combine durability with design.

Best for: Indoor furniture, sculptural pieces, powder-coated designs, architectural installations.

SS - STAINLESS STEEL

The metal that endures. Stainless steel naturally resists rust, moisture, and wear, making it ideal for demanding environments. Strong, refined, and timeless, it delivers durability without compromising aesthetics.

Best for: Outdoor furniture, railings, facades, coastal spaces, and commercial installations.
